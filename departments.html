Programming

Our programming team is responsible for writing the code that brings our robots to life. They work with various programming languages and software tools to ensure our robots operate efficiently.

Design

The design department focuses on creating the physical appearance and aesthetics of our robots. They use CAD software and innovative design techniques to make our robots visually appealing and functional.

Mechanics

Our mechanics department is in charge of building and assembling the robot's physical components. They work with various materials and tools to construct and maintain our robots.
