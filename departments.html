Robot Programming

Our robot programming team is responsible for writing the code that powers our combat robots. They use various programming languages and software tools to ensure our robots operate effectively during battles.

Robot Design

The robot design department focuses on creating the physical appearance and functionality of our combat robots. They use CAD software and innovative design techniques to make our robots formidable and visually striking.

Mechanical Engineering

Our mechanical engineering department is in charge of building and maintaining the physical components of our combat robots. They work with various materials and tools to construct and repair our robots for battles.
